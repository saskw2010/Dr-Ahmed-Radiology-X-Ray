# Modality Checklists

## Ultrasound

- Organ/system examined
- Size, echotexture, focal lesions
- Vascularity when relevant
- Ducts, lymph nodes, fluid, and adjacent structures

## Doppler

- Deep venous system
- Superficial venous system
- Compressibility and flow
- Reflux/insufficiency and perforators when relevant
- Arterial waveform, velocities, stenosis/occlusion when requested
- State clearly whether arterial assessment was performed

## CT

- Contrast status
- Acquisition/reformats
- Target organ and adjacent structures
- Acute complications and limitations

## MRI

- Sequences and contrast
- T1/T2/STIR or relevant signal description
- Location, size, morphology, enhancement, diffusion
- Marrow, soft tissue, neurovascular and joint relationships when relevant

## X-ray

- Views
- Alignment, bones, joints, soft tissues
- Acute fracture/dislocation
- Cardiopulmonary or other system-specific checklist
